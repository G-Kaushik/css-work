Font And Text Properties:


Text Properties:

text-align
left, right, center, justify

text-transform
uppercase, lowercase, capitalise(for all word's first letter to have capital letter)

text-decoration
underline, overline, none, line-through

text indent
a number followed by pixels

letter-spacing
a number followed by pixels

word-spacing
a number followed by pixels

line-hide
a number



Font Properties:

font-family
serif, sans serif, fantacy, monospace, cursive

font-size
a number followed by pixels or rem

font-weight
normal, bold

font-style
italic, oblique, normal
